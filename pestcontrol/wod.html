What Are Wood-Boring Beetles Like?

Powder post beetles, old house wood-borer beetles, and common furniture wood-boring beetles are all examples of “wood-boring beetles.” Adults and woodworms have very similar characteristics. Adult wood-boring beetles can be less than 1 inch long, ranging from 0.25 inch to 0.625 inch (1/4 to 5/8 inch) in length. The length of a woodworm ranges from 0.039 to 0.28 inch (1 to 7mm).

Wood-boring beetles have a variety of coloured bodies. The colors of big powder post beetles are black, and the bodies of adults of old house wood-boring beetles are black or grey. Furthermore, the bodies of adult common furniture wood-borer beetles are brown or black in colour. The woodworms of all wood-boring beetles, on the other hand, have dark heads and white or cream-colored bodies.
