Cockroach Control Tips and Tricks

Here are some simple, effective steps for eliminating cockroaches and mastering cockroach control:

  • Dry foods should be stored in tightly sealed containers or sealed plastic bags.
  • Avoid leaving food on kitchen surfaces.
  • Do not leave liquids in tins, buckets, sinks, or cans.
  • Clean up food waste and spills from food preparation areas regularly.
  • Maintain the cleanliness of trash cans and keep garbage and compost in tightly sealed containers.
  • Rinse and recycle cans, bottles, and plastics.
  • Perform plumbing repairs to prevent leaks and entry points for cockroaches.
